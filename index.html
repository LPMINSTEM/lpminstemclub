About Us

LPM IN STEM CLUB is dedicated to fostering innovation and curiosity in STEM fields. We provide a platform for students to explore, learn, and grow in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics.

Our mission is to inspire the next generation of innovators, problem solvers, and leaders in STEM through hands-on learning, mentorship, and community engagement.
